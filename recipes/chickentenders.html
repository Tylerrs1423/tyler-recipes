Description

Crispy Oven Baked Chicken Tenders! Made with strips of chicken tenders, parmesan and an herb and spice seasoned panko bread crumb finish. They are absolutely delcious!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 1/3 cups panko bread crumbs
  • 2/3 cup (1.7 oz) finely shredded parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 lbs. chicken tenders>/li>
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp minced fresh parsley

Steps to Make Chicken Tenders

  1. Preheat oven: Move oven rack up one level from center and preheat oven to 425 degrees. Spray an large oven safe cooling rack with non-stick cooking spray and set over a rimmed 18 by 13-inch baking sheet.
  2. Prepare dredging ingredients: Pour flour into a small shallow dish. Crack and whisk eggs in a second shallow dish.
  3. In a third dish (such as a pie plate) toss together panko bread crumbs, parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, paprika and garlic powder. Drizzle with olive oil then toss well to evenly coat.
  4. Season Chicken Tenders: Season both sides of chicken tenders with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake to cook through: Bake in preheated oven until chicken is cooked through.
  6. Garnish: If desired, garnish with parsley and serve warm.
