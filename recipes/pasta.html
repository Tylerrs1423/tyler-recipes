Description

This Italian style soup is loaded with hearty ground beef, fresh vegetable. creamy beans, tender pasta and delcious herbs all in a rich and savory broth.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Olive oil
  • Yellow onion
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Kidney beans
  • Great northern beans
  • Low-sodium chicken broth
  • Sugar
  • Dried oregano
  • Basil
  • Thyme
  • Marjoram
  • Ditalini pasta
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps to Make Pasta Fagioli Soup

  1. Cook beef in a pot, drain and transfer beef.
  2. Saute veggies in same pot.
  3. Add broth, tomato sauce, canned tomatoes, sugar, dried herbs and beef.
  4. Simmer until veggies are tender
  5. Cook ditalini.
  6. Add pasta and beans to soup warm briefly then stir in parsley.
