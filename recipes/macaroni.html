Description

This is the aboslute easiest way to make macaroni and cheese! Believe it or not, it's unbelievably creamy, no broken curdled sauce, no mushy noodles, and the taste is simply perfect.

Ingredients

  • Dry macaroni pasta
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Evaporated milk
  • Heavy cream
  • Onion powder and garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Sharp cheddar cheese
  • American cheese
  • Monterey Jack cheese

Steps to Make Crockpot Mac and Cheese

  1. Spray a 5 to 6-quart slow cooker with non-stick cooking spray.
  2. Add macaroni pasta, butter, water, evaporated milk, heavy cream, onion powder, garlic powder and season with salt to tatse. Stir then level and submerge pasta in liquid as best you can.
  3. Working quickly remove lid and add chedar, American cheese and Monterey Jack cheese. Stir then quickly return lid.
  4. Cook covered 15 to 30 minutes longer until pasta is tender and cheese is melted.
  5. Stir. Thin with a little hot water as needed. Season with pepper to taste if desired.
